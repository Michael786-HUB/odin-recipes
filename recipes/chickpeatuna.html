INGREDIENTS

  • Can of Chickpeas | 150g
  • Can of Tuna | 80g
  • Spinach | 100g
  • Soy Sauce (reduced salt)
  • Curry Powder
  • All Purpose Seasoning
  • Stock

STEPS

  1. Mix chickpeas with curry powder, seasoning and stock
  2. Wash spianch and douse with soy sauce
  3. Microwave
  4. Drain oil from Tuna and add to dish
